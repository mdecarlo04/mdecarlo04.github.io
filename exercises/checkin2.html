Your Guide to Oxford, UK

In July 2024, I spent 6 weeks studying abroad in Oxford and I want to share with you all of my favorite places and recommendations for your trip!

Food Recommendations

  • The King's Arm: experience a historic English pub with the best fish and chips I have ever eaten
  • Najar's Place: the most affordable option on the list with delicious options for everyone
  • George and Davis: a cute cafe and ice cream shop to satisfy your sweet tooth
  • Ramen Kulture: perfect for a solo dinner date with yummy noodles and rice dishes

Things to See

  1. Oxford Botanic Gardens
  2. Port Meadow
  3. Bodleian Library
  4. University Parks
  5. Ashmolean Museum

My Favorite Part of Oxford

After spending 6 weeks in Oxford, I found that my favorite part of the city was not the bustling downtown, but was Jericho, a suburb of Oxford. I stayed at Keble College during my stay and Jericho was a short walk away. There are tons of cute cafes, the Oxford Canal, and cute shops all around. My favorite part of Jericho was Port Meadow, which I mentioned earlier. Horses and cows roam freely, but my favorite thing to do was to sit by the Thames River running through the meadow and read. Nothing more peaceful in the world.
