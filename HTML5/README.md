#Audio description

##About Me
My name is Mary DeCarlo and I am a student at UMass Amherst in the process of receiving a certficate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. Not only do we learn how to write grant proposals and memos or how to use different digital appications, like MadCap Flare, but we also spend a lot of time learning about digital accessibility. So much of our lives revolve around electronics and it is important for all systems and websites to be accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities. 

##About my project
One aspect of digital accessibility is using audio description. My team and I spent a significant amount of time researching and writing about what audio description is, why it is necessary, and how to implement it. We focused on:
-What is audio description? What is integrated description?
-Guidance: best practices, tips, examples
-How to add audio description to a video
