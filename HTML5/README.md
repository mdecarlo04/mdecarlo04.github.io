#Audio description

##About Me
My name is Mary DeCarlo and I am a student at UMass Amherst in the process of receiving a certficate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. This certificate spends a lot of time on cultivating our skills in various digital tools, like MadCap Flare, Word, and Illustrator. As we become proficient in these tools, we also talk about ensuring that our digital work is accessible to all, regardless of their abilities. To get our class thinking about the many facets of digital accessibility, we began to work on a project in MadCap Flare detailing different parts of it. 

##About This Flare Project
One aspect of digital accessibility is using audio description. My team and I spent a significant amount of time researching and writing about what audio description is, why it is necessary, and how to implement it. We focused on:
-What is audio description? What is integrated description?
-Guidance: best practices, tips, examples
-How to add audio description to a video
