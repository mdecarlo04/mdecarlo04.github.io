What is PWTC?

PWTC is a five-course certificate in Professional Wiriting and Technical Communication that provides.

  1. practice in teachnical wiriting, editing, and software documentation:
  2. hands-on training in a range of industry-standard software;
  3. an introduction to theories and practices of information desight;

Since 1990, the PWTC program has enjoyed an impressive record of job placement, with graduates embarking on careers in technical writing, user experience (UX), and information design. Some are employed by nonprofits and government agencies, others by firms involved with healthcarem software development and information technology.

Who can enroll?

The PWTC certificate is open to English majors, as well as interested students from other majors. To enroll in PWTC courses, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and at least a junior standing.
